For Medical Tourism Agencies

Why Türkiye for Agencies?

Türkiye is rapidly becoming one of the top destinations in medical tourism, offering high-quality healthcare services, affordable treatments, modern medical facilities, and a high standard of living. With its clean water, low air pollution, rich cultural heritage, and historical attractions, Türkiye provides both medical care and tourism opportunities in one place.

Many internationally accredited hospitals are equipped with the latest medical technology, top-standard laboratories, advanced operating rooms, and highly experienced healthcare professionals, delivering services in five-star hotel comfort.

In healthcare, having the best doctors alone is not enough. The best treatment outcomes come when specialists from different fields work together with a multidisciplinary approach.

What We Do

If you are a medical tourism agency or an international healthcare provider referring patients to Türkiye, we act as your local representative to help you secure the most suitable agreements with hospitals or medical facilities for your patients.

We negotiate on your behalf, assist at every stage of the process, and help resolve any issues that may arise.

Our fees are determined together based on the type and level of support you require.
